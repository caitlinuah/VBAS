Via Stellaris

Monthly Newsletter

“Via Stellaris” is a monthly publication produced by the Von Braun Astronomical Society to update the community and VBAS members on the society’s affairs as well as to inform readers of upcoming events and opportunities for astronomy in the community. Some of the content of “Via Stellaris” may include upcoming events inside the VBAS organization, Huntsville community, and our stunning night sky. Members can also buy and sell equipment using the classifieds or find out more about astronomical literature in the book reviews.

To subscribe to “Via Stellaris” and learn more about the astronomy community at VBAS, using our Contact Page, email us using the subject “Subscribe.” For questions or submissions for the newsletter, use the subject “Newsletter” so that we can respond quickly and efficiently to your message.
