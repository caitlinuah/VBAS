Members meet at our planetarium on the third Friday of each month, except for December, to discuss recent and upcoming astronomy events which usually includes a guest speaker and the public is welcome to attend! We also have planetarium programs every Saturday night that the public is invited to and we enjoy viewing the evening skies afterwards when the weather permits.
