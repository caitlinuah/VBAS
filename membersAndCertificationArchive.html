Messier Observation Program

According to Astroleague.org's "Messier Observing Page," the purpose of the Messier Observing Program is to familiarize the observer with the nature and location of the objects in the sky.

The Messier Observation Program Certificate is designed so that observation explorers of all experience levels can search for the objects listed in the Messier Catalog. These are 110 objects that were cataloged by the French astronomer and comet-hunter Charles Messier so he could keep track of objects that he might later confuse for comets.

There are two levels of the Messier Certification:

  • Certified:
    • 70 Messier Objects
    • Certificate
  • Honorary:
  • 110 Messier Objects
  • Certificate and Pin
