How To Find Our Facilities In Monte Sano State Park

Our "street address" is:
Monte Sano State Park
5105 Nolen Ave. SE
Huntsville, AL 35801

Our facilities are located on a mountain just East of Huntsville in Monte Sano State Park. At the park's entrance, a fee is normally charged unless you tell them that you are going to a VBAS event! Once in the park, follow the road to the left at the first intersection until you reach VBAS' entrance on the left just before the overlook in the park.

Your First Visit

It takes about 15 minutes from Huntsville Hospital and you should plan your first visit to arrive while it is still daylight. Additionally, you should arrive at least 30 minutes before our planetarium programs, which fill up fast!

Easiest Route

  1. From Huntsville Hospital, turn left on Governors (Highway 431)
  2. Stay on Governors for 2.9 miles
  3. Turn left on to Monte Sano Bld. SE
  4. Stay on Monte Sano Bld. for 2.4 miles
  5. Turn right on Nolen Ave. SE
  6. Stay on Nolen Ave. for 1.1 miles
  7. Slight left on Bankhead Pkwy. NE
  8. Stay on Bankhead Pkwy. for 0.2 Miles
  9. Planetarium is on the left

Remember NOT to pay the entrance fee since you are going to VBAS' facilities! Follow the park's main road to the left at the first intersection and watch for our entrance on the left.
